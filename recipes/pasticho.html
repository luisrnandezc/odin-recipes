Venezuelan pasticho

Description

A recipe for Pasticho (Venezuelan Lasagna)! Lasagna noodles are layered with a meat sauce, ham, béchamel, and cheese, then baked until golden.

Ingredients:

  • 500g of Ground Beef
  • 200g Emmental Cheese
  • 200g Parmesan Cheese
  • 5 slices of bacon
  • 15 Sheets of Pasta
  • 100g Butter
  • 100g flour
  • 1 L Milk
  • 3 chives
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 1 Carrot
  • 2 cups of Tomato Paste or Puree
  • 1 tbsp Oregano
  • 1 tbsp Cumin
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp. Nutmeg
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. In a large frying pan, place the bacon, chopped into small cubes.
  2. Once golden and crispy, remove and set aside.
  3. With the same bacon fat, sauté the onion, chives and finely chopped garlic.
  4. After a few minutes, add a little olive oil and add the pepper and carrot.
  5. Sauté all the ingredients together with the ground meat. It is important to break it down and turn it into a golden color.
  6. Once all the elements are cooked, brown the meat well, add the tomato puree, oregano, black pepper and salt.
  7. Integrate all the flavors and wait for the sauce to reduce a little.
  8. Add a cup of water and wait for it to evaporate. Remove from heat and reserve.
  9. For the bechamel you must melt butter in a saucepan over very low heat.
  10. Then add the sifted flour to the melted butter and beat until it becomes a kind of paste.
  11. Pour the liter of milk little by little over the paste, it is very important to beat while adding the liquid.
  12. Increase heat to medium and never stop beating. The mixture will thicken and you should remove it from the heat.
  13. Add black pepper and nutmeg.
  14. It will be time to assemble the Venezuelan pasticho: layer of bechamel, sheet of pasta, meat sauce and repeat the process until the ingredients are gone.
  15. On top, cover with slices of Emmental cheese and grated Parmesan cheese.
  16. Bake at 180°C for 25 minutes. Cover the top of the mold with aluminum foil.
  17. With 10 minutes remaining, uncover and gratin the cheese, there should be a golden and very crispy layer.
